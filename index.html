Our story

A Filipino family, a long way from home — and a whole lot of heart

Our family travelled halfway across the world — from the Philippines, by way of North Carolina — to "the far, far end of the map" here in Invercargill. We opened our doors on the 4th of July, and we've been pouring our hearts into every cup and every bake ever since.

Owner and baker Jexter "Jex" Aguiluz is up at 4:30am every morning, making everything fresh by hand before the doors open. It's a true family affair — and yes, we're all "J"s.
